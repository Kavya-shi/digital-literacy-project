Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the sender before sharing personal information
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Never share OTPs or banking details with anyone

Financial/UPI Safety:
- Always verify UPI IDs before making payments
- Do not scan unknown QR codes
- Check transaction details carefully before confirming payment

- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Keep your devices updated with latest security updates

Reporting:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
